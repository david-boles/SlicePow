Ready to Slice? Help:
Slicing:
 1) Copy your long URL (try ctrl+c)
2) Go to slice.pw
3) Paste URL in the brown box (try ctrl+v)
4) Click the animated sword.
5) Go to slice.pw/your-sliced-url to expand and get redirected to your full URL.


Tags:
 You can add certain tags in front of your URL to change options. These are still in development so check back for changes!
(Currently must be in order with no spaces)

#cyourownslicedurl# -- Allows you to have a custom shortened URL, must be made of alphanumeric characters.
#i -- Makes a sliced URL last for as long as possible.

Examples:
#cyoutube##iwww.youtube.com -- Going to slice.pw/youtube would redirect you to www.youtube.com for years to come.
